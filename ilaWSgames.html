The Card Sharp


It is a disguise kit for the Spy. It takes form of a deck of playing cards, with each of the mercs in place of numbers, with the Ten, Jack, Queen, King cards missing. The main gimmick is that you trade off your invisibility for better disguises. Upon clicking MOUSE1, the player can fake weapon usage of their active disguise while using their active weapon, with each use consuming a third of the cloak meter


Weapon Stats:

  • While equiped:
  • + Allows the user to use active weapon and keep disguise
  • + Cloak meter regeneration rate increased by 10%
  • + Uncloak time and sound reduced
  • - Using weapon while disguised consumes 33% of the cloak meter
  • - Invisibility consumes cloak meter 100% faster
  • - Disguising consumes 33% of the cloak meter
  • - Undisguises once cloak meter hits 0%

Scorch Shot( Rebalance idea )


Main usage: Strictly medium range weapon option for the Pyro as crowd control or as a finisher.


Weapon Stats:

  • While active:
  • Projectiles are fired in an arc
  • + Increased weapon switch speed
  • + Minicrits on burning players
  • + Projectiles explode on surfaces
  • - Weapon does not reload passively
  • - Self damage taken increased by 40%
  • - 20% lower projectile speed
  • - 25% damage penalty
